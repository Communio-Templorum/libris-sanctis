Though we have gathered here a collection of "sacred" texts, we must caution you:
  1. All texts were written by men and speak of the Gods and the universe only to the best that the authors could know at the time of writing.
  2. Each text has been translated, but they represent merely one possible translation. Only the original text in its original language may be considered sacred.
  3. Each text is a product of their generation and cannot be taken as fully authoritative in any other time without consideration of the historical context in which they were written.
